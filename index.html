Emotional Zombie

It's 9:30 in the evening and it wants to be put out of its misery.

If only it were truly alive, then maybe it could die.

It feels all these different emotions but doesn't know which one to trust.

It makes noise with its mouth that it thinks is the truth but knows is a lie.

From the millions of voices within its head, one can be heard at a time.

Speaking on its soap box it tries to direct the narrative of an alternate reality.

If you want to live then get out of harms way.

Stay away from this emotional zombie, stumbling through life, with no free will and not an ounce of genuine compassion.

It will eat your pure heart to replace its own.

Its brain generates a random patch sheet pillow case to try to muffle the haunted screams of its heart's past.

That's maybe why it can only live in the moment or in the far too distant future.

Its dreams, asperations and ideas are not good.

They are a sinking ship.
