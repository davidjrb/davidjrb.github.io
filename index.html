Disembark



If only it were alive, then maybe it could die.

It feels all these different emotions but doesn't know which one to trust.

It makes noise with its mouth that it thinks is the truth but knows is a lie.

From the millions of voices within, one can be heard at a time.

From its soap box it tries to create the narrative of an alternative reality.

If you want to keep your sanity then get out of harms way.

Stay away from this wannabe Cartasian demon.

If you pay attention you'll notice that there's no rhyme or reason to its thinking.

Its driven by its own random self generated emotional illusions.

A fictitious means to justify immediate gratification and fornication.

It drags itself around this world, in search of meaning.

It has no free will or compassion for others.

Once it sinks its teeth into your heart it won't let go.

It hopes to replace its own with the love and courage it recognizes in you.

Its inner voices try in vain to drown out the haunted screams of its past.

That's maybe why it can only live in the moment or in the far too distant future.

Its dreams, asperations and ideas are not good.

They are a sinking boat.

Save yourself.

Disembark before its too late.
